A New Way to Visualize Music Lyrics Throughout the World.

Pop music changes so much throughout the years. Here, we created an innovative way to visualize how lyrics around the world have shifted over time. Our program examines the lyrics of the top ten charting songs of nine countries (including US, UK, and Korea) from 2010 to 2016. It has two components: the world map, which displays the nine countries with colors representing the overall sentiment of the lyrics of that year (___ being ___, and ___ being ___), and the word cloud, where you can see particular lyrics stood out at a specific time and place.
